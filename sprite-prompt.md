Create an original pixel art sprite sheet of a cute retro sci-fi UFO.

Art direction:

- Retro 2D arcade and handheld game aesthetic from the late 1990s
- Fully original UFO design
- No resemblance to copyrighted alien ships from movies, games, or franchises
- Cute, clean, readable pixel-art style
- Bright colorful retro palette
- No logos, text, symbols, or UI elements

UFO design:

- Small rounded flying saucer with chibi proportions
- Dome-shaped glass cockpit on top
- Circular body with soft curves
- Tiny glowing underside lights
- Compact cartoon-like silhouette
- Friendly and playful appearance
- Keep identical proportions, perspective, and structure across all frames

Color palette:

- DO NOT use grayscale-only colors
- Use a vibrant limited retro palette
- Suggested colors:
  - Teal
  - Cyan
  - Purple
  - Lime green
  - Pink
  - Yellow highlights
  - White highlights
- Background must be solid magenta (#FF00FF)
- No gradients
- No anti-aliasing
- Keep palette limited and consistent across all frames

Technical constraints:

- Canvas size: 192 x 96 pixels
- Layout: 3 rows x 6 columns
- Each frame fits exactly inside a 32 x 32 pixel cell
- All sprites must align consistently to the same center point
- Keep the UFO centered in every frame

Direction rule:

- The UFO must always face RIGHT
- Any cockpit detail or front-facing lights must point RIGHT
- Never mirror or flip frames horizontally

Animation consistency rules:

- Maintain identical scale and perspective across all frames
- Keep the UFO body anchored consistently
- Animate only bounce, tilt, lights, beam glow, or tiny hover movement
- Ensure smooth frame-to-frame synchronization
- Preserve silhouette consistency in every frame

Rendering rules:

- Crisp readable outlines
